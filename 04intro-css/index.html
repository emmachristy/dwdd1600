The Parks

There are two different Parks at Tokyo Disney

  • Tokyo Disneyland
  • Disney Sea

Tokyo Disneyland

Tokyo Disneyland, the Kingdom of Dreams and Magic, consists of seven themed lands, each offering fun attractions in line with its theme, as well as a variety of unique shops and restaurants. Have a magical day at Tokyo Disneyland with your Disney friends!

  • World Bazaar : a bustling early 20th century American town center
  • Adventureland : a world of adventure rich in nature and cultural diversity
  • Westernland : America's "Wild West" abounding with frontier spirit
  • Critter County : take a peak into the joyful lives of the critters that dwell here
  • Fantasyland : a world of fairytales and dreams replete with the Disney magic
  • Toontown : a fun-filled neighborhood where Mickey and his friends live
  • Tomorrowland : a land of tomorrow where science and technology are in harmony

Disney Sea

The only Disney park themed to the myths and legends of the sea offers a variety of unique themed ports and attractions as imagined by Disney.

  1. MEDITERRANEAN HARBOR A romantic Southern European port town
  2. AMERICAN WATERFRONT The nostalgia-filled American harbors of New York and Cape Cod
  3. PORT DISCOVERY A futuristic marina located across the horizons of time
  4. LOST RIVER DELTA The ruins of an ancient civilization in the Central American jungles
  5. ARABIAN COAST The world of The Arabian Nights filled with magic and mystery
  6. MERMAID LAGOON An undersea kingdom home to Ariel, the little mermaid, and her friends
  7. MYSTERIOUS ISLAND The eccentric genius Captain Nemo's secret base
